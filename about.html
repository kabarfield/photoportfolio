HELLO! MY NAME IS ALI, I'M THE GIRL BEHIND THE CAMERA.

I'M SO GLAD YOU STOPPED BY MY SITE! GRAB A CUP OF TEA AND TAKE A LOOK AROUND.

If you clicked this page wanting to get to know me better than you're in luck! Here's a few little details about me in a nutshell:

1. Photography is my biggest passion and though I take it seriously, I like to have a lot of fun during sessions.
2. I started my business as a sophomore in high school.
3. I moved to NYC August 2017 for college to study journalism and fashion marketing.

If you want to know even more, here's an expansion! My middle name isn't actually May, that's the month I was born in! I’ve always had a passion for being creative and expressing this through art. I loved drawing and painting until I discovered the world of photography. I began to get serious about photography a couple years ago when I got my first DSLR camera. I did a photography challenge where I took a photo every day for a year and that was when I really saw the magic in capturing those little moments in time that could never be recreated. Ever since then I’ve been photographing friends, families, newlyweds and everything in between.

I’m a lover of coffee and tea. I am completely full of wanderlust and love traveling around the world, from small trips to grand adventures. My favorite part of those adventures is the company. I love the people I travel with and get to meet. Though I was based in NC, I now live in NYC but am available for travel anywhere you are!

I’m so lucky to get to document your moments whether you’re a giddy senior, newly engaged lovebirds or you’re walking down the aisle. I’m here to help you tell your story!

Thank you for taking a look into my world! I can’t wait to be a part of yours.

Feel free to contact me to just say hello or ask for more information about a session!
